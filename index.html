Season 1

Episode 1 - Pilot

We are introduced to Mr. White, a strong man with a big temper, as he begins "Breaking-braincells".

Episode 2 - The Deal

Mr. White establishes his first drug deal; however, things get out of hand as he notices how strong his product truly is.

Episode 3 - The New Dealer

There is a new dealer in town, and Mr. White is met with a very grumpy debt collector.

Episode 4 - The Friend Discount

Mr. White, who now goes by Heisensus, is high up in the world of drugs and business, and we meet Lessie, a high school dropout desperate for cash along with his friend Ingram.

Episode 5 - Revenge

Mr. White meets with his Distributor and makes a deal with him, before rendezvousing with some familiar faces.
