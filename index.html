About

Breaking Brain Cells was born from the creative vision of Jack Trowell and his group of friends in association with Apex Films.

This Breaking Bad-inspired TV series was started in early 2023 as a couple comedic skits but developed into a group passion.

There are Five episodes within the season.

Breaking Braincells series

Season 1

Episode 1 - Pilot

We are introduced to Mr. White, a strong man with a big temper, as he begins "Breaking Brain Cells".

Episode 2 - The Deal

Mr. White (Heisensus) discovers the true nature of his product, and decides to sell it to a drug addict to get his name known.

Episode 3 - The New Dealer

Heisensus gets caught up in a fight with a previous customer and negotiates with a drug Distributor.

Episode 4 - The Friend Discount

Heisensus is high up in the world of drug tafficking and we are introduced to two new characters Lessie and Ingram.

Episode 5 - Revenge

The finale of season 1, Heisensus organises a meeting with the Distributor before he unexpectedly meets a few familiar faces.
